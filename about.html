Brew Lab

Each week, student-brewed beer is available to purchase in our brewery on the KPU Langley campus at great prices! Brewed on a state-of-the-art NSI Newlands pilot brewing system, this is quality beer! Bring your own growler or buy a KPU growler to bring back for a fill each week. Proceeds from beer sales go back into the Brewing and Brewery Operations Diploma program to help support the students' education.

$10 for a 64 oz fill

$6 for a 32 oz fill

*tax included
